# Design

## Palette Strategy
Restrained dark mode. High-contrast white accents against deep black and dark glass surfaces.

## Color Palette (OKLCH)
- `--background`: `oklch(0.08 0 0)` (Pure near-black)
- `--foreground`: `oklch(0.98 0.002 70.0)` (Off-white body text)
- `--surface`: `oklch(0.12 0.005 70.0)` (Dark glass surface)
- `--border`: `oklch(0.20 0.005 70.0)` (Subtle borders)
- `--primary`: `oklch(0.60 0.124 70.0)` (Amber / honey brand seed)
- `--accent`: `oklch(0.45 0.12 280.0)` (Cool dusk-mauve tension)
- `--muted`: `oklch(0.60 0.005 70.0)` (Secondary text)

## Typography
- `--font-sans`: Inter
- `--font-display`: Space Grotesk
- Body Line Length: `65–75ch`
- Display Letter Spacing: `tracking-tight` (min `-0.04em`)
- Headings: `text-wrap: balance`
- Body/Paragraphs: `text-wrap: pretty`
