# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Small businesses, startups, restaurants, agencies, and retail owners looking for high-end digital experiences.

## Product Purpose
Build trust within the first 5 seconds and demonstrate the design and technical excellence of AVENIQ.

## Brand Personality
Confident, modern, minimal, elegant, technical, premium. Never loud or childish.

## Anti-references
Generic templates, cookie-cutter layouts, visual clutter, cheap meta-labels, loud and over-saturated color schemes.

## Design Principles
- **Premium First**: Every detail must feel intentional; use generous white space and clean typography.
- **Motion with Purpose**: Smooth transitions and interactive reveals that enhance storytelling, never for cheap decoration.
- **Simplicity**: High-density layouts (like Bentos) but with minimal UI elements.
- **Content Driven**: Powered by an admin dashboard to easily scale projects.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict contrast ratios for body and interactive elements.
- Keyboard navigation out of the box with custom visible focus states.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` settings in all transitions.
